Bacon Pancakes

Description

Basically, pancakes with bacon stuffed inside, because why not combine two of the best breakfast foods? If you want your morning to be legendary, this is the way to go. Just add bacon and sing about it!

Ingredients

  • pancake batter
  • strips of cooked bacon

Steps

  1. Fry up some bacon until it's crispy (not burnt, but close).
  2. Make your usual pancake batter (flour, eggs, milk â€“ you know the drill).
  3. Pour the batter into a pan, then sneak in a few pieces of bacon.
  4. Cook until golden, flip it like a pro, then stack it all up and eat like a breakfast champion. Bonus points for singing while you cook!
