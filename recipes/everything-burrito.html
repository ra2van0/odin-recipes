Everything Burrito

Description

Imagine a burrito that can hold literally EVERYTHING in the universe. It's the one burrito to rule them all, packed with whatever you can think of – basically an edible bag of wonders.

Ingredients

  • large tortilla
  • rice
  • beans
  • scrambled eggs
  • bacon
  • hash browns
  • sausage
  • cheese
  • guacamole
  • salsa
  • any other fillings you like

Steps

  1. Grab a massive burrito-sized tortilla.
  2. Stuff it with everything in your fridge (seriously, everything – cheese, veggies, leftovers, that half-empty jar of pickles).
  3. Roll it up like you've got zero time for fancy wrapping.
  4. Microwave it or just eat it raw – who's judging? It's Everything Burrito. Anything goes.
